Soups

  1. Minestrone

Ingredients:
olive oil
onion
small zucchini
garlic
dry red wine
dried oregano, basil and/or thyme
diced tomatoes
vegetable or chicken stock
cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
Salt and fresh ground pepper

Method:
Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the onion and cook until translucent, about 4 minutes. Add the garlic the green beans, dried oregano and basil, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and pepper to taste; cook 3 more minutes.Add the diced and crushed tomatoes and the chicken broth to the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium low and simmer 10 minutes. Stir in the kidney beans and pasta and cook until the pasta and vegetables are tender, about 10 minutes. Season with salt.
